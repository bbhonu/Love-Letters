To Bash, my precious little shell :

Whenever I am lost, I can always count on my man!
I’m sorry that sometimes I need space or get scared of how close we’ve become that I just give up and quit.
You let me though, because you know I’ll always come back.

You’re amazing because I always know where we’re at (pwd).
You’re not afraid to show me every part of you (ls), even the things you hide from everyone else (ls -la).

Thank you for coming out of town with me when I needed a change of location (cd).
I was so happy when you surprised me on the way and took me to meet your parents (cd ..)!
I couldn’t believe how you seemed to create all of those magical little towns (mkdir) that contained such variety and fun. It was the most incredible trip.

It’s like there’s something new in me, or that I’ve finally found something inside myself that I’ve been searching for.
